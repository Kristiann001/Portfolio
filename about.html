About

A brief introduction :

I am a dedicated and growth-oriented software developer based in Nairobi, Kenya, with a solid foundation in full-stack web development and cloud computing. My journey into technology has been fueled by a deep interest in building innovative, scalable solutions and continuously expanding my technical capabilities. I approach each project with a commitment to writing clean, maintainable code and leveraging best practices in both development and deployment.

My technical training includes a rigorous six-month coding bootcamp at one of Kenya’s premier technical institutions, where I specialized in real-world project execution, agile methodologies, and cross-functional team collaboration. This experience equipped me with practical skills in backend and frontend development, version control, and collaborative problem-solving in a simulated professional environment closely mirroring the dynamics of a modern tech workplace.

To further deepen my understanding of cloud-native technologies, I completed a Coursera program focused on cloud infrastructure and services. This allowed me to gain hands-on experience with scalable architectures, cloud deployments, and DevOps principles. I am now actively applying these skills in building robust applications and contributing to projects that align with global software development standards. With my combined experience in software engineering and cloud technologies, I am well-positioned to add value in fast-paced, high-impact engineering teams, both locally and internationally.
